This sheet reflects cases, statutes and figures as they were discussed live in the recording. Case names, neutral citations, licence numbers and statutory references marked VERIFY below have not been independently checked against primary legal sources and should be confirmed by qualified counsel before this document is relied upon, cited, or circulated outside LSLC's membership. ev·digi is a content and production partner, not a source of legal verification.

Cases discussed

MatterStatusDetail
Mercuria v The Baltic Exchange VERIFY · ONGOING Commercial Court proceedings concerning the TD3C freight benchmark and the Baltic Exchange's benchmark administration decisions following the Hormuz closure. Expedited hearing listed for October 2026. No neutral citation yet available — confirm court division and claim number before external circulation.
LME v Elliott VERIFY · REPORTED Referenced as the closest precedent: the 2022 London Metal Exchange nickel trading suspension and cancellation of trades, subsequently challenged by judicial review. Described in the session as unsuccessful for the claimants and drawn on as a parallel for how courts approach requests to unwind or reconstitute an entire market. Confirm full case name and citation before external circulation.

Statutes & regulation referenced

InstrumentStatusDetail
UK Benchmarks Regulation RETAINED EU LAW The retained version of Regulation (EU) 2016/1011, governing the administration of and contribution to financial benchmarks as it forms part of UK law. Discussed in relation to the Baltic Exchange's regulatory position and the FCA's role as designated competent authority. Referenced by the panel as conferring no private right of action.
General Licence (referenced as "General Licence X") VERIFY · SANCTIONS Sanctions relief instrument referenced in connection with the post-ceasefire environment. Discussed as not extending automatically to IRGC-linked or otherwise separately designated entities. Confirm the specific licence number and issuing authority (OFAC/OFSI/EU) before external circulation.
